About Vishaka

Vishaka is an Cultural Club which is mainly designed to represent Telugu people in KARE.

Our Mission

The programs by Vishaka is dedicated to serve the development and enhancement of educational and cultural activities within the campus and in reach of fully inter-Disciplinary in scope. We envision a center that provides opportunities, exchange of ideas and skills between the campus to creative activities in Education art, culture, literature performance.
